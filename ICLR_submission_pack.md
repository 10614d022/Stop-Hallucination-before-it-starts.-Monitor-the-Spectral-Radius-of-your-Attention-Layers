# ICLR / NeurIPS Reviewer-Facing Pack

## Paper positioning
This work is a **testable causal hypothesis paper**, not a completed empirical benchmark.

## Main claims
1. We propose a measurable spectral instability signal.
2. We provide an intervention protocol.
3. We define explicit falsification criteria.

## Reviewer defenses
### Why no full benchmark?
Because the paper's contribution is the framework + falsifiable protocol.

### Why is this publishable?
Because it converts an informal failure phenomenon into a measurable and intervenable object.

### What would falsify it?
- No lead-time
- No collapse reduction under intervention
- Quality collapse without stability gain

## Safe abstract sentence
We do not claim causal proof; we provide a framework that enables direct causal testing.
