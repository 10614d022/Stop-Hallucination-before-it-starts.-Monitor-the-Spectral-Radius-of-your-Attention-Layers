## Theorem-level

1. **Theorem A (Upper Bound on Local Coupling)**
   - 若 \(r \gg n^{-2/3}\)，則局部鄰域與 GW 過程在 TV 距離下收斂。

2. **Failure Boundary Theorem (Lower Bound)**
   - 若 \(r \le n^{-2/3-\delta}\)，則存在 witness event（diamond）使 TV 不收斂。

3. **Sharp Threshold Theorem**
   - 
   \[
   d_{TV} \to 0 \iff r \gg n^{-2/3}.
   \]
